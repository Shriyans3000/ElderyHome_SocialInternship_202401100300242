‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How can I schedule a visit to Seva Ashram?

You can schedule a visit by calling us at +91 98765 43210 or filling out the contact form above. We welcome families to visit and see our facilities firsthand.

What is the admission process?

Our admission process involves an initial consultation, health assessment, facility tour, and discussion of care requirements. We ensure the best fit for both the resident and our community.

Do you provide medical care 24/7?

Yes, we have qualified medical staff available 24/7, including trained nurses and on-call doctors for emergencies and regular health monitoring.

Can families visit regularly?

Absolutely! We encourage family visits. Our visiting hours are flexible, and we have comfortable spaces for families to spend quality time with their loved ones.

What activities are available for residents?

We offer yoga, meditation, cultural programs, festivals, music therapy, gardening, arts & crafts, and social activities to keep our residents engaged and happy.
